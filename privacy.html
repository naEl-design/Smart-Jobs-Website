Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026 | Effective date: April 1, 2026

✅ Key summary: We collect only what's needed to run the platform. We never sell your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

When you use smartJobs Rwanda, we collect:

  • Account information: Name, email address, password (hashed), and role
  • Profile information: Phone number, location, professional skills (optional)
  • Job applications: Application messages and the jobs you've applied to
  • Job listings: For employers, the job postings you create
  • Usage data: Pages visited, search queries (anonymized)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Displaying relevant job listings and connecting seekers to employers
  • Routing you to the correct dashboard based on your role
  • Sending application confirmations and status updates
  • Powering AI features (CV builder, job match assistant)
  • Maintaining platform security and preventing fraud

3. How We Share Information

  • With employers: When you apply for a job, your name, email, phone, location, and cover message are shared with that employer only.
  • With seekers: Employers' company name and job details are visible to all job seekers.
  • Service providers: We use Anthropic's Claude API for AI features.

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

4. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion: Request permanent removal of your account and data

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@smartjobs.rw.
