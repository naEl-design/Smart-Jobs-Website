Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using smartJobs Rwanda

Getting Started

How do I create an account?
Click "Sign In" in the top right corner, then select "Create Account". Choose between "Job Seeker" or "Employer", fill in your name, email, and password. Your account will be ready instantly!
Is smartJobs free to use?
Yes! smartJobs is completely free for job seekers. Employers can post jobs for free as well. Premium features for employers will be available soon.
Can I use smartJobs on mobile?
Absolutely! Our platform is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.

For Job Seekers

How do I apply for a job?
Browse jobs on the home page, click any job card to view full details, then click "Apply Now". Fill in your contact information and cover message, then submit your application.
How do I track my applications?
Log in to your Job Seeker Dashboard. You'll see all your submitted applications with their current status: New, Reviewed, Shortlisted, or Rejected.
How do I use the CV Builder?
Go to "AI CV Builder" from your dashboard. Enter your name, headline, summary, and skills. The preview updates live. Click "Export PDF" to download your professional CV.
Can I save jobs for later?
Yes! Click the bookmark icon on any job card to save it to your "My Bookmarks" section. You can access saved jobs anytime from your dashboard.

For Employers

How do I post a job?
Sign in as an Employer, go to your Employer Dashboard, and click "Post a Job". Fill in job details including title, description, salary, industry, and location. Your job goes live immediately.
How do I manage applications?
Your Employer Dashboard shows all applications received for your jobs. Use the status dropdown to mark candidates as Reviewed, Shortlisted, or Rejected.
Can I edit or delete a job posting?
Yes! In your Job Listings table, click "Edit" to modify job details or "Delete" to remove the listing entirely.

Technical & Account

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
On the Sign In page, click "Forgot password?" and enter your email address. A reset link will be sent to you within minutes.
How do I change my email or phone number?
Log in and go to your dashboard. Click on "My Resume" or "Profile Settings" to update your contact information.
How do I delete my account?
Email support@smartjobs.rw from your registered address with the subject "Account Deletion Request". We'll process your request within 2 business days.
