For Job Seekers

How do I create an account?
Click "Sign In" in the top right corner, then select "Create Account". Choose "Job Seeker" as your role, fill in your name, email, and password.
How do I apply for a job?
Browse jobs on the home page, click any job card to view details, then click "Apply Now". Fill in your details and submit.
How do I use the AI CV Builder?
Go to "AI CV Builder" from your dashboard. Enter your name, headline, summary, and skills. Click "Export PDF" to download.
Is smartJobs free?
Yes! smartJobs is completely free for job seekers. You can browse, apply, and use the CV builder at no cost.

For Employers

How do I post a job?
Sign in as Employer, go to Employer Dashboard, click "Post a Job". Fill in the job details and submit — it goes live immediately.
How do I manage applications?
Your Employer Dashboard shows all applications. Use the status dropdown to mark as Reviewed, Shortlisted, or Rejected.

Technical & Account

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
On the Sign In page, click "Forgot password?" and enter your email address. A reset link will be sent to you.
Does smartJobs work on mobile?
Yes! smartJobs is fully responsive and optimized for mobile browsers.
How do I delete my account?
Email support@smartjobs.rw from your registered address with the subject "Account Deletion Request".
